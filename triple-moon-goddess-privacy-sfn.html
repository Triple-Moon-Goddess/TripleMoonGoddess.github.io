Privacy Summary

Your data,
simply explained.

This is a plain-language summary of how Triple Moon Goddess handles your information. For the full picture, see our Privacy Policy.

What we collect
  • Birth date, time & location — to generate your natal chart
  • First name or nickname — stored separately from your health data
  • Email address — for account access, chart delivery, and reminders only
  • Daily health journal entries — mood, energy, sleep, symptoms, food, notes
  • App preferences — reminder times, house system, food plan, tab config
Users aged 13–17: a limited natal chart experience is available with parental consent. No account is created, no email is collected, and no data is written to any server. The chart is stored on your device only. All other TMG apps require users to be 18 or over.
How we use it
  • To generate and display your natal chart and astrological insights
  • To power your health journal and track patterns over time
  • To send reminders and deliver PDF exports you request
  • To provide optional AI-generated summaries and conversation (requires your own API token after 5 free uses — you control this)

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. Ever.

Who we share it with
  • Google Firebase — our database, authentication, and hosting infrastructure
  • Google Cloud Run — hosts our astrological calculation API (birth data only, not linked to your identity)
  • Anthropic — only when you use AI features; receives journal context only, never your name, email, or birth data
  • Google Workspace — used to send reminder emails and PDF exports
  • Law enforcement — only if required by law or valid legal process

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. All service providers are contractually bound to process your data only on our instruction.

How we protect it
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Your health data and identity are stored in separate database collections — no single record contains both
  • Practitioner apps use client-side encryption for clinical notes and client emails — unreadable without the practitioner's passphrase, even to us
  • API keys stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager, never in code
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict every user to their own data only
Your controls
  • Delete all your data at any time — Settings → Danger Zone
  • Disable AI features at any time — remove your API token in Settings → AI
  • Request a copy of your data, correction, or deletion by emailing us
  • EU/EEA/UK users have full GDPR rights (access, erasure, portability, restriction, objection)
  • California users have full CCPA/CPRA rights
We never
  • Sell your personal data
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your health data to train AI models
  • Combine your identity with your health records in a single database document
  • Send client names, emails, or birth data to any AI processor
  • Access device contacts, calendar, camera, or location
