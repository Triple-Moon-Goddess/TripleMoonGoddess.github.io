Common questions

Which consultation is right for me?

If you're working with patterns in relationships, identity, work, or the inner life — Personal Growth. If you're working with physical patterns like fatigue, digestion, sleep, autoimmune symptoms, or hormonal cycles — Physical Health. If you suspect both are connected — the Bundle gives you both at a saving.

I don't have a birth chart yet. Can I still book?

Yes. The booking link takes you to the system that will guide you through creating your chart — birth date, birth time, and birth location are required. Once your chart is on file, you complete your intake form and book your session.

What if I need to pause the intake form?

The intake form is saved by your email. You can pause and resume at any time. We recommend completing it at least 48 hours before your session so Lisa has time to review it alongside your chart.

Can I reschedule?

Yes — reschedule through the booking confirmation email. For bundles, sessions need to maintain the minimum spacing (7 days for the Growth + Health bundle, 14 days for the 3 Physical Health bundle).

Is this covered by insurance or FSA?

No. Astrological consultations are not covered by insurance, and they are not FSA-eligible expenses. Payment is made directly at booking.

What if I'm not sure I'm ready for the deep work?

The Guided Onboarding Intake Call at $100 is the right starting place. We walk through filling out your intake form together — no prep needed, just bring yourself. You decide afterward whether to book a full consultation.
