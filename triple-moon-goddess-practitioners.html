Privacy by design

Three layers of separation: the database schema, the encryption key only you hold, and your paper journal.

Schema-Level Pseudonymization

Charts, journals, intake answers, and practitioner notes each live in their own table, joined only by access code. No clinical table contains a name or email.

Client-Side Encryption

Practitioner notes, psychological readings, and the client email you enter are encrypted in your browser with a passphrase only you know. TMG cannot decrypt this data — the key never leaves your device.

No Backdoor, No Recovery

Your encryption passphrase is never stored or transmitted. If you lose it, the encrypted data cannot be recovered — not by you, not by Lisa, not by anyone. Recovery mechanisms are attack surfaces. We don't have one.

Email Lives Alone

If a client provides an email, it lives in a single identity table apart from every clinical table — encrypted at rest with your passphrase. Remove that one record and the clinical data becomes fully anonymous.

Your Paper Journal

The link from access code to your client's full identity — name, phone, address — lives in your paper journal, in your office, under your professional control. TMG never holds it.

Practitioner Isolation

Every clinical record is stamped with your practitioner ID. Other practitioners cannot read your notes, results, or exclusions — enforced at the database level.

Common questions

Which app is right for me?

If you’re an astrologer or integrative practitioner who wants the full TMG methodology with editable libraries and astrology surfaced — the Integrative Practitioner App. If you’re a licensed therapist or psychologist working with personal growth patterns — the Soul Pattern App. If you’re a health practitioner or medical doctor working with constitutional health and remedies — the Constitutional Health App. The two clinical apps don’t surface astrology language at all.

What if I want both Soul Pattern and Constitutional Health?

The Integrative Practitioner App at $100/mo includes both modules along with the full astrological framework, editable libraries, and orb settings. Subscribing to both clinical apps separately would cost $300/mo. Practitioners who want the full picture choose the Integrative app.

Can I try the platform before subscribing?

Yes — schedule a free 1-hour video call with Lisa to walk through the platform together. No card required, no commitment. Email Lisa@TripleMoonGoddess.com to schedule.

How does the AI dialog feature work?

The AI dialog is optional in all three apps. You bring your own Anthropic API key — Anthropic bills you directly (~$5/mo). TMG never holds your AI billing. The onboarding hour walks you through setup.

Can I switch between monthly and annual later?

Yes. Both plans can be switched at any time from your account portal. Stripe prorates the difference automatically.

Does TMG see my clients’ names or contact information?

No names, no phone numbers, no addresses. The only identifying information TMG holds is an email address — and only if your client chooses to provide one. Email lives in its own identity table, completely separate from every clinical table.

How does the encryption work?

When you log in, you enter a passphrase that derives a 256-bit encryption key in your browser. That key encrypts your practitioner notes, psychological readings, and the client email you enter — before they're ever written to the database. The key never leaves your device. TMG cannot decrypt your data, and there is no recovery mechanism if you lose the passphrase. The cryptography is XSalsa20-Poly1305 (authenticated encryption) with scrypt key derivation — the same standards used by NaCl/libsodium.

Can other practitioners see my client notes?

No. Every note, intake result, exclusion, and reading you write is stamped with your practitioner ID and visible only to you. Enforced at the database level — not a setting that can be turned off.

Is TMG an FSA-eligible expense?

No. TMG is a SaaS subscription for your practice — a deductible business expense, not a qualified medical expense.

Does my subscription include readings or sessions with Lisa?

No. Subscriptions give you access to the TMG platform. Lisa’s individual services are scheduled and paid separately on the main TMG website. The onboarding hour is a platform training session, not an astrological reading.

What happens to my client data if I cancel?

Important: download what you need before cancelling. While subscribed, you can download per-client PDFs of questions, responses, outcomes, and your clinical notes. Once you cancel, account access ends immediately and your account, custom questions, notes, and platform records are deleted. Pseudonymized chart and response data remains in the research corpus.
